About

PurposeThe Toy Exchange is a free platform for toy swaps and giveaways in your area.
BackgroundParents Helping Parents is a non-profit organization that offers a support system for parents in their journey in parenthood. Some of our past projects include seminars on parenting, education, college applications, financial planning, etc. We have also implemented an online babysitting co-op program that connects parents with babysitting needs in the community. Our latest project, The Toy Exchange, aims to facilitate the sharing of resources through toy swaps and giveaways.
Age GroupThe toys are intended for preschool to school-age children ages 0 to 10.

Why Use the App

This app is designed for parents whose children have outgrown or lost interest in their toys. Rather than purchasing new toys, download this app to:
  • Keep it fresh and fun with age-appropriate toys free of charge
  • Give your pre-loved toys a new home
  • Instill values of sharing and thoughtful giving in your children
  • Be evironmental friendly through practices of reduce, reuse, and recycle
